Sunay Joshi

     

Hi! I'm a PhD student at UPenn interested in statistics, ML, reliable AI, and AI-for-math. I am grateful to be advised by Prof Edgar Dobriban and Prof Hamed Hassani. I received my AB in math from Princeton.

I am also fascinated by single-cell RNA sequencing, structural biology, manifold learning, optimal control, mathematical statistical mechanics, the foundations of statistics, mathematical and statistical finance, the interface of statistics and entrepreneurship, inventing more interactive, continuouus, and natural modes of human-AI creative collaboration, creating personalized educational content using AI, and designing scalable and inclusive academic workflows for our collective AI-empowered future.

My email address is sunayj [at] sas.upenn.edu. Please reach out, I'd love to learn from you and collaborate! My desk is in Amy Gutmann Hall.
